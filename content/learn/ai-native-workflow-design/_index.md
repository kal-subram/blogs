This series is about one specific workflow I designed, starting from customer research and ending with a hi-fidelity Figma design of an AI-native version of it. I am keeping the specifics of what the workflow actually does light, since the underlying work was done at Amazon and a lot of the detail is confidential. What I can share is the process, the tools, and what I would do differently.

The short version of the context: this was a safety and compliance workflow in e-commerce, the kind of process that used to need a person to look at almost every case. My job was to figure out whether, and how, that could become AI-native without losing the judgment a human was providing.
