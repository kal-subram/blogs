This is where the training series live, each one written for a PM, not an engineer. Some come with real, runnable code you can try yourself. Others walk through an actual project end to end, the research, the tools, and the decisions, not just the slides version of it.

Two series so far, more on the way.

1. PyTorch for PMs, six short posts on the machine learning basics a PM actually needs
2. Designing an AI-Native Workflow, nine posts on taking a manual compliance process to an AI-native design, from customer research to a final Figma screen
